About TalentFlow

TalentFlow is a smart job aggregation platform designed to help students discover relevant job opportunities from multiple sources in one place. Instead of visiting different job portals, users can explore curated listings collected through automated job scraping.

This project was developed as part of an academic initiative under the Centre for Science of Happiness, focusing on building technology that simplifies the job search process.

Our goal is to make job hunting faster, smarter, and more efficient by providing a centralized platform where students can easily find opportunities based on role, location, and skills.
